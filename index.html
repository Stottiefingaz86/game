How to play

  • Lanes: ← → or A D · mouse left / center / right · on phone: drag or swipe left/right on the game
  • Jump: space / up / W · click / tap the game (not the HUD)
  • Backward: S or ↓ · on phone: while touching the game, drag mostly downward (hold) — max 10 m behind best distance; release to run forward
  • Duck: auto while holding backward on the ground — slip under low beams
  • Score: coins × meters
